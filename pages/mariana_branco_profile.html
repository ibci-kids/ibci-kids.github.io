Lab Description

At the Utrecht-BCI Lab we aim to understand the relationship between electrical, metabolic and cerebrovascular processes of neuronal populations during normal and abnormal behaviour. In addition, we focus on the development of fully implanted home-use Brain-Computer Interfaces for individuals with communication disabilities.

Research Interest Keywords

sensorimotor cortex, fMRI imaging, intracranial signals (ECoG and sEEG), communication BCIs, developments, children and adults.

Current research topics

  • Imaging brains of children and adults with quadriplegic cerebral palsy (qCP)
  • Identification of needs and interest in BCIs for Cerebral Palsy using user-centered design
  • Development of BCIs that work 24/7
